EzUptime, the Uptime Robot

2021-03-01 by Battledash2

EzUptime is the best uptime robot! You don't need to signup.. You can also choose whether to use curl or ping while pinging your site.

EzUptime was made for everyone to keep their codes always-on. Have you ever hosted something on repl.it or heroku and needed to keep it online? Well that's why this exists!

EzUptime is easy to use AND requires no signup, which makes it easier to use!

There are no limits on how many sites you want to ping! Just head over to the create page and enter in your url! Your project will be pinged every 5 minutes.

Why do we have no signup?

We understand that people don't like sharing personal information with sites they don't know, so we've decided to not add a signup! It's more convenient for all of you.

The only question is.. Why didn't you create your uptime yet? Head over to the create site and create one!
